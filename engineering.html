PASS ENGINEERING

Pass Engineering

PASS is an iso 9001: 2015 Certified Company, one of the leading structural fabrication and Machined parts manufactures in Kerala, developing a fully equipped facility in Pazhanji, Thrissur, Kerala. We operate in a total area of ​​16000 sqft. - covered area of ​​7000 sqft., open area of ​​8000 sgft. and office area of ​​1000 sqft. In construction sector we are specialized in the steel structural fabrication, canopy structure, steel mezzanine floor, outer fencing, spiral ladder, cat ladder, safety bollards, handrails, curtain wall brackets, trash skip and loading platforms.

In industrial sector we are specialized re-in forced hinges etc. In food industries and Hospital sector we are using various grades of stainless steel as per customer requirements. we are specialized in manufacturing of processing table, packing table, drainage grill, manhole cover, refrigeration trolley, pallet converter, and tanks with material certificate. We provide the solutions in the projects; we can ensure timely project completion with end to end quality management and budget control.

Our every employee believes Value Engineering is an important part of our work.
